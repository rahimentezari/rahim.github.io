AutiGaze- An application for Autism Spectrum Disorder

I've been always thinking about using machine learning for medical purposes. Fortunately, my wife is studing occupational therapy and so I am somehow familiar with Autism Spectrum Disorder(ASD).

Autism is a pervasive developmental disorder,first described in 1943 by Leo Kanner, chracterized by a triad of impiarments:

- social comppunication problems

- difficulties with reciprocal social interactions

- unusual patterns of repetetive behavior

Did you know that?

These facts motivates me to know more about ASD, especially the current situation in Iran:

So, my wife and I decided to design a smartphone application, AutiGaze, which will be used by children with autism. This application help them to improve their social behaviors.

why is this a good solution?

- Easily accessible for almost evetyone, instead of looking for doctors and visiting occupational therapists.

- The striking role of smartphone in people's life specially in Iran.

- Children with ASD like to play with digital devices and there is no need to force them.

- It can be used any moment,any place.

- lowering the cost in the phase of rehabilitaion.

- Pervasive usage helps screening in large scale.

- It can be fine-tuned for othe disorders like ADHD.
